HOW TO PLAY

CONTROLS

  • Arrow Keys: Move Left/Right/Down
  • Up Arrow: Rotate Piece
  • Spacebar: Hard Drop (Instantly drop piece)
  • START/PAUSE: Toggles the game state

SCORING

  • 1 Line: 100 Points
  • 2 Lines: 300 Points
  • 3 Lines: 500 Points
  • 4 Lines (Tetris): 800 Points

LEVELING UP

Clear 10 lines to advance to the next level. Each level increases the falling speed of the pieces!

OBJECTIVE

Complete horizontal lines to clear them from the board. The game ends if the blocks stack up to the top of the screen.
