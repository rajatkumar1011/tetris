HOW TO PLAY

CONTROLS

  • Arrow Keys: Move Left/Right/Down
  • Up Arrow: Rotate Piece
  • Spacebar: Hard Drop
  • START/PAUSE: Toggles game

SCORING

  • 1 Line: 100 Points
  • 2 Lines: 300 Points
  • 3 Lines: 500 Points
  • 4 Lines (Tetris): 800 Points

LEVELING UP

Clear 10 lines to advance to the next level. Each level increases the falling speed!
